Flan

ik the ingredient lean more crème caramel less mexican flan but w/e i think it's good this way so

ingredients

for flan

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 1/4 cups half n half
  • 4 oz crème fraîshe
  • 3 eggs + an extra yolk
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • a bit of nutmeg
  • a lil salt

for caramel

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

directions

put 4 eggs uncracked in a warm water bath -- it'll help the flan cook a tad quicker

preheat oven 325 F

in saucepan combine sugar/water, set low heat, move pan around but don't stir for ~10 min or until the mixture is amber

immediately pour caramel into flan tin

if you're feeling fancy grate a tiny extra bit of nutmeg onto the caramel

blend all flan ingredients

to reduce air bubbles run mixure through a fine sieve, and maybe give the flan tin a solid tap on the counter -- you can also scoop some bubbles off the top but the top will be the bottom and who's really checking that? if you're worried google how to reduce air bubbles

put your flan tin into a smaller tin with water about halfway up the tin -- if you're in a rush add less water

if your flan tin doesn't have a cover, cover it w/ tin foil

bake for like ~1.5-2.5 hours, or until internal temp reaches 175 F -- it should still jiggle but not look liquidy anywhere

don't stay up till 3am making this ♡

enjoy!!
