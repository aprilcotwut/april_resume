Mac n Cheese

ingredients

for sauce:

  • 4-7 big cloves
  • 1/2 onion
  • small shallot (optional)
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 1 1/2 - 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1-2 tsp paprika
  • 1-2 tsp cayenne powder
  • white pepper (or just pepper) to taste
  • ~ 1 cup gouda cheese
  • ~ 1 cup gruyere cheese
  • ~ 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • fresh ground pepper to taste (optional)
  • salt

for pasta:

  • salt
  • 16 oz pasta

directions

mince or blend onion, then shallots and garlic, reserving 1/2 of garlic.

add butter to pan and turn on low/medium heat, add flour to prior pan. stir, and let flour cook ~5 minutes to create a kind of roux - let it slightly brown.

begin to boil water for pasta

add onions, garlic and such to pan and cook until translucent and you stop crying

add in spices except reserved garlic

add 1 1/2 cup milk + heavy cream stir for a while till homogeneous

hopefully your water is boiling - add pasta, salt, and some oil if you partake of that religion

add in equal parts of each cheese to sauce, reserving some (1/2ish) for topping

add in the garlic set asid.

if the sauce is too think add in up to another 1/2 cup milk

mix w noods

top with each of the cheeses reserved - if you have a lasagna dish you can possibly even add a middle layer of cheese

top w panko, cook for like 15-20 mins or so

enjoy!!
