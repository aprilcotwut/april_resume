Creamy Asparagus Soup

ingredients

  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1/2-3/4 yellow onion
  • 10 garlic cloves
  • seasame oil (or other oil)
  • 3ish tbsp miso paste
  • thyme (1/2 fresh bunch or like 2 tsp dried)
  • rosemary (1/2 fresh bunch or like 2 tsp dried)
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 1-2 cups no chicken broth (imagine) or water + more miso
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 1-2 tsp fresh ginger
  • 12 oz neutral coconut milk (Aroy-D) or other cream of choice (crema, half n half, creme fraishe)
  • 3 tbsp verde salsa (or sub for more lime)
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt
  • msg
  • pepper

notes

makes like 4-5 servings, best served w/ bread

this is basically a twist on a recipe by nasim

directions

dice onion, asparagus, and garlic (no need to mince)

cook onion in oil until clear, then add miso paste and a splash of broth/water or dry white wine if you have it

add in thyme and rosemary (both optional, with thyme more important) and chopped asparagus (ends trimmed ofc) and cook a bit more

blend with remaining broth, coconut milk or cream, parsley, and ginger, altho save a bit of parsley for garnish

put the blended mixture back over stove and add bay leaf then salt, msg, pepper, lime to taste -- if you want save like 1/2 the lime to slice and serve w/ soup

put your bread in the oven!!! cook the soup for like 10-15 mins or until your bread is done and you're impatient.

person who gets the bay leaf has to do dishes :x

enjoy!!
