Hummus

ingredients

  • 1 15 oz can chickpeas or like 3/4 cup dried chickpeas
  • baking soda (optional)
  • 5.5+ oz tahini (i rec soom)
  • garlic (measure w your heart... maybe 5 cloves?)
  • 1 small shallot, or like 1/2 a big boi
  • lemons/limes (1-2ish) or their juice
  • za'atar / some sumac spice blend
  • salt
  • good olive oil (lucini, california olive ranch)

directions

prep chickpeas in the way you wish, either soak overnight, or cook and do the baking soda method (google!) - at the very least cook until the skins have come loose

peel the skins best you can or until you're sick of doing so - kinda like poblanos, i feel like the most important thing is cooking them until the skins are soft enough to peel

boil a bit of water on the kettle

for a lil twist, bake your garlic n shallots on the comal a bit if you wanna

food process or blend your chickpeas, garlic, shallots, tahini, and some warm water as needed

add a bit of olive oil, salt, za'atar, and lemon to taste n blend more

prep n make a lil divot to add more olive oil and za'atar

if you want, you can further top w/ parsley, paprika, grilled shallots/garlic, etc

enjoy!!
